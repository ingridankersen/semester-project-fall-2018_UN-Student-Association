Who we are

Here at UN Student Association Oslo, we are driven by a single goal; to bring attention to important issues that impact our global society and ensure the preservation of the universal human rights for all. We strive to impact the world at large by creating a sustainable world in which there is international solidarity, peace and cooperation.
